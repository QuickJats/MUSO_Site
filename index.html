What is MUSO?

MUSO is an RPG about exploring all the dream worlds put together in something referred to as the 'Dream Stone'.
Explore these worlds and seal the creatures invading Earth away as an average schoolboy who loves archery named Atabi with the help of Nuttalli, Markus, and Casey.
Do good deeds to benefit yourself by getting items or increasing stats.
