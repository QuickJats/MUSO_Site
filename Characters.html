Hailing from Greenthum, Atabi is a no so smart, sarcastic 14 year old who loves archery. He may be weak but he can shoot a bow!

Markus is often known as the kid spending time in the flowers and constantly having his head in the clouds. Atabi being his best friend. The two have been through a lot together.

Coming from what Atabi refers to as 'The Flip-Down', Nuttallii is a rebellious 15 year old who despises anything her mom tries to make her wear. Even if she's tough, she's not too great with magical abilities.

Casey is a detective, and is good friends with Atabi's father, Adam. Despite living on his own in an apartment in Greenthum, he's often called into Bluesbird to investigate on crimes.
